Why Us

We promise we will work our hardest to help your student improve their ACT score.

One Stop Solution

We're your one stop solution to all things ACT. No need to shop around. If you have any ACT-related questions, we have an answer.

Online

We offer classes online and in-person. For in-person, we will come to your home (or another location of your choosing). For online, we teach through Zoom.

Guaranteed Improvement

We guarantee your student's score will improve as they work through our unique program. Our track record is stellar.

Services

Options and Price Points

  • 1-on-1 Tutoring

    Specifically designed to fit your student's needs, our 1-on-1 tutoring allows us to focus on your student's weak points. We charge $50/hour. We can tutor at your home or another location.

  • Group Tutoring

    We offer a five week course for groups ranging from 6 to 10 students. We meet weekly for an hour and a half with our final meeting consisting of a full-length test. The cost is $300 per student, however, if you form your own group, you can receive a discounted rate of $250 per student.

  • School-Sponsored Tutoring

    We offer large group courses at select high schools in Utah. For details on if we serve your high school, please reach out to us or your administration.

  • Online

    We also offer our 1-on-1 tutoring as well as our group tutoring online through Zoom.
